## Risks & Mitigation

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Adobe builds competing features
- *Mitigation:* First-mover advantage, deeper customization, multi-catalog support Adobe won't prioritize

**Risk:** Niche market limits growth
- *Mitigation:* Multiple products, different markets, diversified revenue

**Risk:** Subscription competitors undercut pricing
- *Mitigation:* One-time pricing is competitive advantage (customers prefer it)

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** Local AI not powerful enough
- *Mitigation:* Models improving rapidly (Llama 3.2, Qwen 2.5), already viable

**Risk:** Support burden overwhelms
- *Mitigation:* Comprehensive docs, video tutorials, community forum

**Risk:** Platform dependencies (Lightroom, Ollama)
- *Mitigation:* Support multiple LLM backends, plan for alternatives

### Business Risks

**Risk:** Marketing doesn't reach customers
- *Mitigation:* Strong organic channels (Reddit, forums), low CAC needed

**Risk:** Refund rate too high
- *Mitigation:* Free trials, clear documentation, responsive support

**Risk:** Legal issues (medical advice, photo liability)
- *Mitigation:* Legal review complete, disclaimers, insurance
